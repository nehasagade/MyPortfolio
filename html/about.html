About Neha Sagade

I'm a professional currently based in Sydney, Australia. As an artist, I use traditional mediums - mostly watercolour - and I like to draw everyday objects or scenes as well as illustration. I'm inspired by the Japanese language and culture and I spent four years living in Japan. I also exhibited at the Nagoya Foreign Artists' Exhibition in 2020. You can see the complete range of my art on my Instagram, @sixforts. For professional enquiries, please contact me via LinkdIn.
